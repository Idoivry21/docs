Everything you need to pick a plan, pay for it, and keep track of usage. Billing lives in [Settings → Billing](https://hearflow.io/settings/billing).

## Plans

Hearflow is priced per workspace. Every plan includes:

- All 13 integrations and CSV Upload
- AI extraction, clustering, and the Insights dashboard
- Unlimited team members
- Stripe-backed billing and invoices

Plans differ by monthly AI budget, historical backfill window, sync cadence, and support SLA. Starter polls each connected source once a week on your configured [sync day](/settings#sync-cadence); Pro and Enterprise poll daily. See the [pricing page](https://hearflow.io/pricing) for the current tiers.

## Starting a subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a plan">
    From [Settings → Billing](https://hearflow.io/settings/billing), click **Upgrade** on the plan that fits your usage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Stripe checkout">
    You'll be redirected to a secure Stripe checkout page. Enter your payment details and billing address — Stripe handles all card data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Hearflow">
    After checkout, you'll land back in Hearflow with the new plan active. A receipt is emailed to the billing contact on file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing billing

Use the **Manage billing** button in Settings to open the Stripe customer portal. From there you can:

- **Update your payment method** — Add, remove, or replace cards.
- **Download invoices** — Access every past invoice and receipt as PDF.
- **Update billing address & VAT ID** — Tax details propagate to future invoices.
- **Cancel the subscription** — Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

## Usage metering

Each plan has a monthly AI budget. Hearflow tracks how much of that budget has been spent on extractions, dedup, and quality checks.

- Usage resets on your billing anniversary each month.
- You'll receive email notifications at 75% and 95% of the budget.
- When the budget is exhausted, ingestion continues but AI extraction is paused until the next cycle — or until you upgrade.

## Trials

<Check>
  **14-day trial on paid plans** — New workspaces get a 14-day trial of a paid plan. Trials require a card on file; you're charged at the end of the trial unless you cancel. Downgrade to the free tier at any time during the trial without being charged.
</Check>
