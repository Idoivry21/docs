Everything about adding people to your workspace and controlling what they can change.

## Inviting teammates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team settings">
    Go to [Settings → Team](https://hearflow.io/settings/team). You'll see a list of active members and pending invitations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter emails">
    Paste one or more email addresses (comma- or newline-separated) and pick a role for each. Invitations are sent immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invitation expiry">
    Invites expire after 14 days. Expired invites can be resent in a single click without creating a new one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Roles

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Owner" icon="crown">
    Full control over the workspace, including billing and deletion. Every workspace has exactly one owner at a time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Admin" icon="shield-check">
    Can manage integrations, invite members, and change workspace settings. Cannot change billing or transfer ownership.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Member" icon="users">
    Read and interact with insights. Cannot change integrations, settings, or billing.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Managing invitations

- **Resend** — Click resend on any pending invite to deliver a fresh copy of the email.
- **Revoke** — Delete a pending invite to invalidate the link. The invitee can no longer join with it.
- **Remove members** — Admins and owners can remove active members. Removed members immediately lose access.

## Transferring ownership

<Warning>
  **Transfer before removing the current owner** — Ownership can be transferred to any existing admin. The previous owner is demoted to admin. Transfer happens immediately and cannot be undone without a reverse transfer.
</Warning>
