Sync Asana tasks and comments as structured feedback signals.

## Prerequisites

- An Asana workspace and project with customer-facing feedback
- Permission to authorize Asana via OAuth

## Connect Asana

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations in Hearflow">
    Go to [Settings → Integrations](/settings) and select Asana.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete OAuth authorization">
    Grant access to your Asana workspace in the consent flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose projects and scope">
    Select which projects to sync and confirm setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Sync Works

**Task ingestion** — Tasks and comments are combined into transcript-like records.

**Backfill** — Historical project activity is imported after configuration.

**Ongoing sync** — New task changes are captured in scheduled sync runs.

<Note>
**Data access** — Hearflow reads only authorized projects and stores OAuth credentials encrypted at rest.
</Note>
