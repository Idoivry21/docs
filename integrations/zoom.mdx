## Prerequisites

- A Zoom account (Pro, Business, or Enterprise)
- Cloud Recording enabled in your Zoom settings
- Audio transcription enabled for recordings

<Note>
  Hearflow only processes recordings that have transcripts available. Make sure transcription is enabled in your Zoom settings before your meetings.
</Note>

## Connecting Zoom

Follow these steps to connect your Zoom account to Hearflow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Log in to your Hearflow account and go to [Settings](https://app.hearflow.io/settings) from the navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Integrations Section">
    Scroll down to the **Integrations** section where you'll see available data sources including Zoom.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Zoom">
    Click the **Connect** button next to Zoom. This will redirect you to Zoom's authorization page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Hearflow">
    Review the permissions requested and click **Allow** to authorize Hearflow to access your Zoom recordings and transcripts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Backfill Begins">
    After authorization, you'll be redirected back to Hearflow. We'll automatically start importing your recent recordings (last 60-90 days) in the background.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Connection Successful** — Once connected, you'll see a green "Connected" badge next to Zoom in your Settings. Your first insights should appear within a few hours depending on the number of recordings.
</Info>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial Backfill">
    When you first connect, Hearflow imports your cloud recordings from the last 60-90 days. We download the transcripts (VTT files) and process them to extract insights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Daily Sync">
    Every 24 hours, Hearflow automatically checks for new recordings and processes any that have completed transcription. No manual action required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Analysis">
    Our AI analyzes each transcript to identify product insights including feature requests, pain points, bugs, pricing feedback, usability issues, workflow gaps, and blockers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insight Clustering">
    Similar insights are automatically grouped into clusters. Each insight shows frequency (how many times it's been mentioned) and includes evidence quotes from the original transcripts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Data Access & Privacy

Hearflow requests only the minimum permissions needed to function:

<Note>
  **What We Access**
  - **Cloud Recordings** — list of your cloud recordings and their metadata (title, date, duration)
  - **Transcripts** — VTT transcript files associated with recordings
  - **User Profile** — basic profile info (name, email) to identify the connection
</Note>

**What We Do NOT Access**

- Video or audio files (we only process transcripts)
- Live meetings or real-time data
- Your contacts or calendar
- Other users' accounts or data

<Info>
  **Security & Encryption** — All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). OAuth tokens are stored encrypted using industry-standard practices. We never store your Zoom password. See our [Privacy Policy](https://hearflow.io/privacy) for complete details.
</Info>

## Managing Your Integration

### View Connection Status

Go to [Settings → Integrations](https://app.hearflow.io/settings) to view your Zoom connection status. You'll see:

- Connection status (Connected/Disconnected)
- Last sync time
- Number of recordings processed

## Disconnecting Zoom

You can disconnect your Zoom integration at any time. Here's how:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to [Settings](https://app.hearflow.io/settings) in your Hearflow dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Zoom Integration">
    Scroll to the **Integrations** section and locate Zoom.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Disconnect">
    Click the **Disconnect** button. A confirmation dialog will appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Disconnection">
    Confirm that you want to disconnect. This will revoke Hearflow's access to your Zoom account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **What Happens When You Disconnect**
  - Hearflow will no longer sync new recordings from your Zoom account
  - Existing insights extracted from your recordings will remain in Hearflow
  - You can reconnect at any time to resume syncing
  - To delete all your data, contact [support@hearflow.io](mailto:support@hearflow.io)
</Warning>

### Removing from Zoom Marketplace

You can also remove Hearflow directly from your Zoom account:

1. Log in to [zoom.us](https://zoom.us)
2. Go to **Settings → Apps** (or visit the Zoom App Marketplace)
3. Find Hearflow in your installed apps
4. Click **Uninstall** or **Remove**

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No recordings appearing?">
    Make sure cloud recording and transcription are enabled in your Zoom settings. Recordings may take a few hours to process after the meeting ends.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Connection expired?">
    OAuth tokens can expire. Simply click "Reconnect" in your Settings to refresh the connection.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Missing transcripts?">
    Hearflow only processes recordings that have transcripts. Ensure audio transcription is enabled before your meetings in Zoom settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
