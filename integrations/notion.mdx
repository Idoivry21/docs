Connect Notion to sync feedback pages and product context docs.

## Prerequisites

- A Notion workspace with pages or databases containing customer feedback
- Permission to authorize a Notion internal integration

## Connect Notion

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start from Integrations">
    Go to [Settings → Integrations](/settings) and choose Notion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize the workspace">
    Complete OAuth and pick the Notion workspace to connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose sources and purpose">
    Select pages/databases and set each source as feedback extraction or enrichment context.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Sync Works

**Source mapping** — Field mapping keeps document structure consistent during ingestion.

**Initial backfill** — Selected pages are imported after setup and normalized for extraction.

**Incremental sync** — Changed pages are synced in scheduled polling runs.

<Note>
**Data access** — Hearflow reads only connected Notion sources and stores credentials encrypted with AES-256 at rest.
</Note>
