Sync ClickUp tasks and comments as structured product-feedback signals.

## Prerequisites

- A ClickUp workspace with tasks that include customer context
- Permission to authorize ClickUp through OAuth

## Connect ClickUp

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Hearflow integrations">
    Go to [Settings → Integrations](/settings) and select ClickUp.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize OAuth access">
    Complete the consent flow and grant required ClickUp scopes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose workspace scope">
    Select the relevant workspace or lists and save setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Sync Works

**Task ingestion** — Tasks and comments are normalized into records for extraction.

**Initial backfill** — Historical tasks are imported after setup is completed.

**Ongoing sync** — New updates are captured in scheduled sync runs.

<Note>
**Data access** — Hearflow reads only authorized ClickUp data and stores OAuth credentials encrypted at rest.
</Note>
